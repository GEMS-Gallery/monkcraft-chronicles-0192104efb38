Specializations

Brewmaster

Brewmaster monks are tanks who use their mastery of chi and powerful brews to avoid damage and protect their allies.

Mistweaver

Mistweaver monks are healers who use a combination of ancient mists and spiritual energy to mend wounds and restore health.

Windwalker

Windwalker monks are agile melee damage dealers who harness their chi to perform rapid strikes and powerful kicks.

Roles

Tank (Brewmaster)

Brewmasters excel at damage mitigation and control, using their brews to avoid incoming attacks and protect their allies.

Healer (Mistweaver)

Mistweavers are versatile healers, capable of both single-target and area healing through their mastery of mists and chi.

DPS (Windwalker)

Windwalkers are swift melee damage dealers, using a combination of martial arts and chi manipulation to defeat their foes.
